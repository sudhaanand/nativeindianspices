Turmeric (Curcuma longa)

Turmeric is native to South India where tropical heat and moisture facilitate its growth. Turmeric is used both as a spice and Ayurveda medicine due to its anti-inflammatory and antibiotic properties. Curcumin the active component of turmeric gives it the rich yellow pigmentation and has also been known to be an effective deterrent to Cancer causing agents.

The Turmeric root is the often consumed portion of the plant with the roots being dried and powdered to prolong shelf life and ease of cooking. All parts of the Turmeric plant find its use in cooking.

Turmeric is also used in skin care routines due to its antibiotic and skin enhancing properties.
