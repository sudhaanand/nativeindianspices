Indian Bayleaf (Cinnamomum tamala)

Indian Bayleaf is different from its counterparts grown in Europe and has a very strong spicy aroma. They grow well on the Himalayan slopes of India and are often found in the wild.

They are a unique part of the North Indian Mughal cuisine and impart a royal note to the dishes. They are used often in rice dishes, curries, and spice powders.
