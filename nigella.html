Nigella (Nigella sativa)

Nigella are small black triangular seeds with a pungent bitter flavor that are grown in central India. They are a very powerful antioxidant and also helps treat asthma, headaches, influenza, and other winter diseases.

They are used as a seasoning on breads, pickles and in snack mixes and more popular in North Indian cuisine.
