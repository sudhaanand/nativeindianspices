Mustard (Brassica juncea)

Mustard seeds grown and used in India are mostly the brown/black variety with a very pungent flavor. The yellow variety is not as widely used as it is in the Western world.

The oil extracted from mustard is the oil of choice in the Northern parts of India and imparts a unique taste to the dishes. In cooking mustard is used as a seasoning or in spice blends.
