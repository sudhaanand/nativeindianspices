Cumin (Cuminum cyminum)

Cumin is a 4000 year old spice which finds its place in not only Indian cooking but also in Mediterranean, Middle Eastern and Mexican cuisines as well.

Cumin seeds have a strong aromatic odor, and an earthy, savory, slightly bitter taste. They can be used dry roasted, saut√©ed in oil, ground, sprinkled and combined with other spices in spice mixes.

Cumin seeds are excellent for digestion and can be boiled with water to aid in digestion.
